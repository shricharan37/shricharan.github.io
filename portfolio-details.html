About Studentverse Wrapped

Studentverse Wrapped is a feature designed to offer students a personalized summary of their academic performance at the end of each semester, much like Spotify Wrapped provides a yearly overview of listening habits. It includes intriguing insights and highlights based on various academic metrics such as attendance records, CGPA, SGPA, and other relevant data.
